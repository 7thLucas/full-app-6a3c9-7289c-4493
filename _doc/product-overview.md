# Product Overview — AI Brand Manager

## What It Is
AI Brand Manager is a visual workspace app for small businesses that acts as a dedicated AI marketing manager. It generates campaigns, content plans, brand guidelines, social media posts, and full marketing strategies — all from a single, organized workspace.

## Target Users
Small business owners and operators who lack a dedicated marketing team and need a consistent, professional brand presence without the cost of an agency.

## Core Problem
Small businesses struggle to maintain brand consistency, plan content systematically, and execute marketing campaigns without dedicated marketing staff. The result: scattered assets, inconsistent messaging, and zero strategic direction.

## Core Solution
An AI that thinks like a marketing manager — not just a content generator. Users describe their business and goals; the AI produces structured campaigns, on-brand content, and strategic plans. A visual workspace keeps everything organized and actionable.

## Product Scope (MVP)
- **Campaign Builder**: AI-generated marketing campaigns with goals, messaging, and channel plans
- **Content Calendar**: Visual calendar for scheduling and tracking content across channels
- **Brand Guidelines**: AI-authored brand voice, color, tone, and messaging rules for the business
- **Social Media Post Generator**: On-brand posts for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X
- **Marketing Strategy Generator**: AI-produced go-to-market and channel strategies

## Visual Workspace
The app surfaces four primary views:
1. **Campaigns** — active and draft campaigns with status and performance
2. **Content Calendar** — monthly/weekly calendar of planned content
3. **Brand Assets** — guidelines, logos, color palettes, tone-of-voice docs
4. **Performance Metrics** — campaign and content performance at a glance

## Positioning
- **Category**: AI-powered marketing co-pilot for small businesses
- **Differentiator**: Acts as a strategic marketing manager, not just a content writer — produces plans, calendars, and brand systems alongside individual pieces of content
- **Tone**: Confident, professional, approachable. Business-first, not tech-first.
- **Tagline**: Your always-on marketing manager.

## Strategic Principles
- Every output is on-brand and grounded in the business's own brand guidelines
- Strategy precedes content — campaigns and plans come before individual posts
- Visual organization over lists — the workspace is the product
- Small business speed — outputs are fast, practical, and ready to use immediately
