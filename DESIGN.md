# Brander Design Guidelines

## Color Palette
Deep purple/violet primary — modern, trustworthy, creative SaaS aesthetic.

## Typography
- Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans (bold, confident)
- Body: Inter (clean, readable)

## Elevation & Surface
- White card surfaces on light gray background
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Purple accent for CTAs and active states

## Components
- Sidebar navigation with icon + label
- Card-based workspace panels
- Full-screen modal for content generation
- Inline AI chat bubble for strategy advisor
- Calendar grid view for content planner
- Status badges (Draft / Scheduled / Published / Active)

## Feel
Clean, focused, no clutter. Feels like Notion meets Canva — visual, organized, powerful.
