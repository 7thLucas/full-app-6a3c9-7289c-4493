# Brander — AI Brand Manager for Small Businesses

## Product
Brander is an AI-powered brand management platform that acts as a full marketing manager for small business owners who have no dedicated marketing team.

## Users
Small business owners who handle all marketing themselves — no marketing background required. They need clarity, speed, and consistency in their brand and content output.

## Core Problem
Small businesses struggle with brand consistency, creating campaigns, planning content, writing social posts, and building a coherent marketing strategy — all without a dedicated marketing hire.

## Brand & Tone
Professional yet approachable. Confident, action-oriented. Feels like having a smart marketing co-founder, not an agency.

## Core Features (MVP — all present on day one)
1. **AI Campaign Builder** — generate full marketing campaigns from a brief (goal, audience, duration)
2. **Content Calendar** — visual monthly/weekly planner with AI-generated content suggestions per slot
3. **Brand Guidelines Creator** — define and store brand voice, colors, fonts, logo, mission statement
4. **Social Media Post Generator** — create ready-to-publish posts for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
5. **Marketing Strategy Advisor** — AI chat that gives strategic recommendations based on business context
6. **Performance Dashboard** — track campaign status, content completion %, scheduled posts

## Strategic Principles
- Everything generates in seconds — no blank page moments
- Owner sees their whole brand in one place
- AI does the heavy lifting; owner just approves or edits
